COMPANY NEWS

18-07-2019

The Ministry of Energy of Kazakhstan announced plans to export more than 1 billion kWh. to the Uzbek power system by the end of this year.

At the end of June of this year, for the first time in many years, export supplies of Kazakhstani electricity to Uzbekistan. This was reported by the press service of the Ministry of Energy of Kazakhstan.

So, according to the agreement concluded between the authorized organizations of the two countries, more than 1 billion kWh will be exported to the Uzbek energy system during 2019.

One of the important conditions for the export of electricity to Uzbekistan, first of all, is to meet the domestic needs of Kazakhstan.

In order to ensure the reliability and uninterrupted power supply of consumers in Kazakhstan, a set of measures is envisaged for setting up emergency automation (SAON - special automatic load disconnection) and introducing restrictions for Uzbek consumers in the event of technological violations.

Export deliveries of electricity have begun, and to date (from the end of June to 17 July), 145 million kWh have been sold to Uzbekistan.

Export deliveries of Kazakhstani electricity became possible due to the existing surplus of electric power, obtained as a result of the policy of the Government of Kazakhstan on the timely modernization, reconstruction and expansion of power plants in Kazakhstan.
