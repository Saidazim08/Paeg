Modern circuit breakers eliminate short-circuit currents with the shortest possible time delay. They do not resist the action of electrodynamic forces. To destroy their impact manifestation, other technical solutions based on the operation of reactors are used.

The term "Reactor" is used to refer to devices operating due to the manifestation of the forces of various reactions, when a response is created to the course of a certain process, for example, biological, chemical, electrical.
