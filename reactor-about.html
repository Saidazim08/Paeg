6-35 KV OIL THREE-PHASE SHUNTING REACTORS TYPE RTM

Three-phase shunt reactors with natural air cooling RTM are designed for parallel connection to the network in order to compensate for the capacitive current. The reactors are designed for long-term outdoor operation.

The nominal value of climatic factors is in accordance with GOST 15150-69 and GOST 15543-70 for location category 1, version U and UHL.
Installation altitude above sea level, no more than 1000 m.
Operating temperature range from -40 ° С to + 45 ° С.
Seismic overloads on the MSK-64 scale - 6 points.
Voltage class - 6-35kV.

The reactor is equipped with the following instrumentation:

-a gas relay for visual monitoring of the evolved gas, as well as for taking an oil sample;
-oil indicator to control the oil level in the tank;
-safety valve for emergency relief of high pressure inside the tank;
-a thermometer of the "TKP" type to measure the temperature of the oil;


The reactors consist of an active part, a cover and a welded rectangular tank. On the cover there are inputs, an expander with an oil indicator, a gas relay. The active part of the reactor consists of a core and windings. The core of the reactor consists of a magnetic circuit and structural elements that ensure the mechanical strength of the magnetic circuit and the entire active part. The structure of the frame provides the necessary crimping of the winding, reliable fastening of the insulation.A rod-type magnetic circuit having three rods and two end yokes assembled from sheets of cold-rolled electrical steel. The rods are assembled from inserts consisting of electrical steel sheets. Electrical steel sheets are pressed in with glass bands and glued with epoxy glue. The inserts are insulated from each other and from the end yokes with insulating mechanically strong gaskets.
